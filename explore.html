Our Mission & Vision

Claires Organisation Kenya stands as a beacon of hope in Dandora Phase 5, Embakasi North, Kenya. As a Community Centre and an educational institution, we are proudly managed and sponsored by Claires Organisation Kenya (NGO) in partnership with Claires Community Centre (CBO) and Twins International.


(children with jiggers rescued from dandora dumpsite by school director)


Our commitment to excellence is reflected in our impressive 95% literacy rate among students. The remaining 5% consists of newly recruited children from the Dandora dump-site, whom we welcome with open arms and dedicated support to help them catch up with their peers.

We offer comprehensive education through our three main sections: Early Childhood Education (ECD), Primary School, and Junior Secondary, all following the government-approved Competence Based Education (CBE) curriculum.

Our Impact Programs

Feeding Programme: We provide breakfast for ECD students and lunch for all 900 students. This crucial program helps retain children in school, preventing them from scavenging for food at the Dandora Dumpsite, and significantly enhances academic performance. For many of our students, school meals represent their only guaranteed meals of the day.

Child Sponsorship: Through our partnership with Twins International, 450 children receive comprehensive support including tuition fees, school uniforms, feeding programs, and infrastructure improvements. This sponsorship program is vital for breaking the cycle of poverty in our community.
